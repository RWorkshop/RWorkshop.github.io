The R command pbirthday() computes the probability of a coincidence of a number of randomly chosen people sharing a birthday, given that there are n people to choose from. Suppose there are four people in a room. The probability of two of them sharing a birthday can be computed as follows: 


> pbirthday(4)
[1] 0.01635591
